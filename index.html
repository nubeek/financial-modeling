The Problem

How much will I make?

Franchisors are legally prohibited from providing financial performance representations outside of Item 19 of the FDD.

Prospective franchisees need to build a financial model to gain confidence in the model, calculate payback period and help them secure funding.

The Solution

Sell-side research for franchisees

Wefranch works with franchisors to understand their business model, and our modeling experts develop a template to allow prospective franchisees to input their assumptions and see how the business could perform based on their inputs.

Not Financial Performance Representations

Wefranch is not the franchisor or its representative, and is thus able to assist prospective franchisees in their research. We are compliant with franchise laws as we never publish financial performance representations.

Instead, we provide an interactive tool that requires the prospective franchisee to input their assumptions and generate a unique scenario.

This tool increases time to close by allowing franchisees to see the level of execution they would need to produce to meet their goals and reach top-quartile performance.

Decimal is a franchisor that has adopted this tool. Decimal started franchising in 2022 and already has three franchisees open.
